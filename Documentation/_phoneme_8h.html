class  Phoneme
 A Phoneme is a fundamental sound within a language. Combinations of phonemes are responsible for creating the pronunciation of words. For example, the english /b/ phoneme is responsible for the <beh> sound in the word <b>ad. This Phoneme class is for storing those phonemes and certain traits they have. More...
